Integration by parts formula for continuous semimartingales $X$ and $Y$:
\begin{align}
  &X_t Y_t = X_0 Y_0 + \int^t_0 X_s \D Y_s + \int^t_0 Y_s \D X_s + [X,Y]_t\notag\\
  &X_t &= X_0^2 + 2 \int^t_0 X_s \D X_s  + [X]_t\notag
  \label{}
\end{align}
